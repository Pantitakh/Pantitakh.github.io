About Us

SpaceBooker is a web application developed by second-year students of the Digital Media Engineering (DME) program as part of our Studio3 project. Designed to meet the diverse needs of anyone within the Engineering Faculty—students, staff, faculty, and visitors—SpaceBooker streamlines the process of finding and booking rooms, providing a user-friendly experience for everyone.
